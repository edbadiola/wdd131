Background

Reanalen Cabigayan is a dedicated health and wellness advocate from Tacloban City. She founded the Barangay Wellness Hub with the aim of guiding individuals towards a healthier and more balanced life. Her vision is to make wellness accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances. Through this initiative, she strives to promote a sustainable and natural approach to health that focuses on long-term well-being rather than quick fixes.

Her mission is to empower others by sharing valuable knowledge about natural healing, proper nutrition, and self-care. She believes that true wellness is a combination of mental, physical, and emotional health, and she works tirelessly to educate her community on how to maintain this balance. Over the years, she has helped many families and communities embrace a lifestyle that prioritizes wellness, using natural remedies and high-quality wellness products. By encouraging individuals to take charge of their health, she aims to create a more holistic approach to living a fulfilling life.
